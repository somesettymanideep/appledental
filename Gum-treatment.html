Gum Treatment

Healthy gums are essential for maintaining strong teeth and overall oral health. we provide comprehensive gum treatment services to prevent, manage, and treat gum-related issues, ensuring your gums stay healthy and your smile radiant.

Gum diseases, also known as periodontal diseases, are infections and inflammations affecting the gums and supporting structures of the teeth. Common conditions include:

Gingivitis: The early stage of gum disease, characterized by red, swollen, and bleeding gums.

Periodontitis: An advanced stage where gums pull away from the teeth, leading to bone loss and potential tooth loss if untreated.

Signs You Might Need Gum Treatment
  • Swollen, red, or tender gums.
  • Bleeding gums during brushing or flossing.
  • Persistent bad breath.
  • Loose teeth or changes in your bite.
  • Pus between the teeth and gums.
